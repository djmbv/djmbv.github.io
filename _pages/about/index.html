Welcome to my web page! I am a Ph.D. student in Mechanics under the supervision of Prof. François Gallaire in the Laboratory of Fluid Mechanics and Instabilities at EPFL; currently on a mobility stay with Prof. Sylvain Courrech du Pont in the Laboratoire Matière et Systèmes Complexes in Paris. My research interests lie at the intersection of the hydrodynamic stability of thin-film flows, coupled to heat and/or mass transfer, and geo-morphogenesis.

I am originally from Sofia and presently based between Paris and Lausanne. Before starting my doctorate, I earned my Bachelor's degree in Physics from Université de Bordeaux, followed by a Master's degree in Fundamental Physics and Applications from Université Côte d'Azur.
